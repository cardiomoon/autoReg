# Remission survival times of 31 leukemia patients

This data is subdata of anderson with medium(2.3 \< logWBC \<= 2.96) and
high WBC count(logWBC \> 2.96)

## Usage

``` r
anderson2
```

## Format

A data.frame with 31 rows and 6 variables

- time:

  survival time in weeks

- status:

  censoring status 1=failure 0=censored

- sex:

  sex 0=Female 1=Male

- logWBC:

  log white blood cell count

- rx:

  treatment status 1=control 0 =treatment

- WBCCAT:

  WBC count group 1=medium 2=high

## Source

David G. Kleinbaum and Mitchel Klein. Survival Analysis. A Self-Learning
Text(3rd ed)(Springer,2012) ISBN: 978-1441966452
